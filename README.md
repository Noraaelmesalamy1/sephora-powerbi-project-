# Sephora Power BI Project  
Sephora Data Analysis project using Power BI ‚Äì Insights into products, ratings, and sales trends  

---
## Project Overview
This project analyzes **Sephora products and customer reviews** using Power BI.  
The goal is to uncover insights about product performance, customer preferences, and overall trends in the beauty industry.  
This project was created as part of my **Data Analytics portfolio** to demonstrate skills in:
- Data cleaning (Excel , power query)
- Data visualization (Power BI)
- Storytelling with data
  ## Dataset
- Source: [Kaggle - Sephora Products and Reviews](https://www.kaggle.com/)  
- Size: ~50,000 reviews across 3,000+ products  
- Columns: Product ID, Brand, Category, Rating, Review Text, Price, etc.

- **Power BI** ‚Üí Data modeling, dashboards, and DAX measures  
- **Power Query** ‚Üí Data cleaning, transformation, and preparation

## Key Insights

- Strong satisfaction (**4.06/5**, 83% recommendation).  
- **Hero products** (Est√©e Lauder, The Ordinary, Sephora Collection) drive engagement.  
- **Skincare + Makeup** lead (56%), while **Hair & Fragrance** are growth opportunities.  
- Ratings are stable, but **seasonal demand** suggests boosting year-end campaigns.  
- Gaps in **shade inclusivity** and **oily-skin products** highlight areas for expansion.

## Hero Products
![Hero Products]<img width="919" height="508" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/de86b4ea-6864-4b2f-81f1-edf66b956faf" />

**Product Insights:**  
- **Est√©e Lauder Double Wear Foundation** ‚Üí Strong hero product with high ratings and a wide customer base.  
- **The Ordinary Serums** ‚Üí Affordable yet high-performing, showing customers value price-to-performance balance.  
- **Sephora Collection** ‚Üí Private label is competitive and drives strong engagement.  
**Customer Loves:**  
- Everyday essentials like **blush, setting powder, brow wiz, and lipsticks** dominate customer favorites.  
- This indicates Sephora is trusted not just for prestige skincare but also for **staple daily makeup items**.  
**Strategic Opportunities:**  
- Expand availability of **hero products** and **everyday essentials** to maximize revenue.  
- Leverage **The Ordinary** and **Sephora Collection** as value-for-money anchors in marketing campaigns.  
- Maintain a balance between **luxury and affordability** in future product launches.

## Customer Analysis
![Customer Analysis]<img width="980" height="591" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9aa94d79-67e0-4596-90f8-6abb9895f0aa" />


- **Skin Tone:** Light/Medium tones dominate reviews, while darker tones are underrepresented ‚Üí need to expand **shade inclusivity**.  
- **Skin Type:** Combination skin shows highest satisfaction; **oily skin** has fewer reviews and mixed ratings ‚Üí opportunity for targeted solutions.  
- **Recommendation Rate:** 83% recommend products, but 17% do not ‚Üí further analysis needed to address dissatisfaction drivers.
